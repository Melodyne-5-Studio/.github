# Melodyne Studio – Professional Audio Editing & Pitch Correction Software

## About Melodyne Studio
Melodyne Studio represents the pinnacle of professional audio manipulation technology, offering unparalleled access to the individual notes within polyphonic audio material. Unlike traditional audio editors that treat a recording as a single waveform, **Melodyne Studio** utilizes groundbreaking Direct Note Access technology to identify and separate individual notes, chords, and sounds within a complex audio file. This allows producers, sound designers, and mastering engineers to edit pitch, timing, and phrasing with the same intuitive control as editing MIDI data.

With **Melodyne Studio**, the creative potential is limitless. You can correct the intonation of a vocal take without altering the natural character of the performance, or completely rearrange the harmonies within a guitar chord. **Melodyne Studio** serves as the ultimate tool for sound design, enabling users to dive deep into the harmonic structure of their recordings. Whether you are working on a complex film score, a pop production, or a detailed audio restoration project, **Melodyne Studio** provides the clarity, precision, and sonic quality required for professional results. Its integration into modern digital audio workstations is seamless, making **Melodyne Studio** a staple in studios worldwide for achieving polished, emotionally resonant audio.
